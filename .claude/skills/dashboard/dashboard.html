First-time setup

The dashboard fetches data live: Alpaca for your account, and your local persistence/ directory for snapshots and configs. Two one-time grants are needed.

1. Alpaca paper credentials

Stored in your browser's localStorage only. Never sent anywhere except Alpaca's own API.

2. Pick the repo's project root

Grants this page read+write access to your persistence/ and persistence/config/ directories. Stored as a FileSystemDirectoryHandle in IndexedDB; never uploaded anywhere.
